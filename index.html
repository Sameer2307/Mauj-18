About Us

MAUJ'18 (Hostel Day) is one of the events in college which is celebrated for all the people residing in hostel. This includes the involvement of hostel students along with the wardens of all the hostels.

Events

What We Do.

Various events were conducted for the hostel day. All the events were divided into 2 domains which include Sports & Cultural events. Further there were some special events. For all the events. a registration link was created for the students for registering. First of all Intra Block events were conducted for the all the hostel blocks and the conduction of Intra Block events was managed by the respective wardens of all the blocks, organisers and block coordinators. Also, for the faculties the same events were conducted.
